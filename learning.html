Resources

Eager to learn? Follow these links to fantastic resources!

Free Code Camp

Free Code Camp covers a very wide variety of topics and coding languages. They post tons of great content and their tutorials are a wonderful place for a new coder to begin their journey!

Solo Learn

Solo Learn is another FANTASTIC and FREE resource available. Follow along at your own pace to brush up on languages you may not be comfortable with or dive into a new langauge.

Geeks for Geeks

Geeks for Geeks is a site that I recommend to someone who is farther along in their coding journey. There is so much content on this site, I don't know where to begin! Blogs, courses, articles, etc. Beginners can absolutley learn from the articles or courses however there are a plethora of practice problems for the more advanced coder as well.
